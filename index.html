How I work

Problems I solve

Every project I've worked with had a version of the same three blockers. Here's how I clear them.

Problem 01

"We can't get the right exchanges to talk to us."

I have active relationships inside exchanges. I get your project in the room, positioned correctly, and taken seriously — not lost in a cold intake form.

Problem 02

"We launched a pool but nobody's providing liquidity."

Pools fail when incentives are wrong or timing is off. I structure gauges, coordinate bribes through incentive markets like Hidden Hand, and make sure LP rewards actually attract capital.

Problem 03

"We're live but nobody knows it."

Distribution is a BD problem, not just a marketing one. I identify the right exchange partners, co-marketing angles, and ecosystem integrations that give a launch the reach it can't build alone.
